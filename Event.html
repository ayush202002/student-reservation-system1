    Ecstasy Annual Cultural Festival

    Ecstasy is the annual cultural fest of Central Institute of Technology, Kokrajhar, Assam. Being one of the largest cultural fest in entire lower Assam which witnessed various national and international artists. Spread over 3 days and 3 nights, Every year it is conducted at the end of February.
